② Semantic
Primitives를 참조. 의미 기반 이름.

— color/brand/*
— color/text/* (default, muted, inverse)
— color/bg/* (page, surface, overlay)
— color/border/*
— color/status/* (success, warning, error, info)
— elevation/*
— radius/*
③ Component
Semantic을 참조. 컴포넌트별 전용 토큰.

— button/* · input/* · badge/* · card/* · sidebar/* · header/* · modal/*
— 컴포넌트 상태별: default / hover / active / disabled / focused
